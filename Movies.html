Movies
Whether it could be in a theater or at home alone, watching some high quality Anime Movies has always been something i, myself, am found of. The world of Movies has a myriad of beatutifuly made shows to choose from, Whether it can be visually or story-wise. So down below, i've come with a list of one of my top choises and my Go-To's when it comes to Anime Movies.

Your Name


is a 2016 Japanese animated romantic fantasy film written and directed by Makoto Shinkai, produced by CoMix Wave Films and distributed by Toho. Follows the story of Two teenagers share a profound, magical connection upon discovering they are swapping bodies. Things manage to become even more complicated when the boy and girl decide to meet in person.

The Disappearance of Haruhi Suzumiya


is a 2010 Japanese animated drama film. Continuing the story of The Melancholy Of Suzumiya Haruhi, On December 18th, Kyon arrives at school and finds that everything has changed. Haruhi and Itsuki Koizumi are missing, Ryoko Asakura has mysteriously returned, Mikuru Asahina doesn't recognise him, and Yuki Nagato is an ordinary human.

A Silent Voice


is a 2016 Japanese animated coming of age psychological drama film based on the manga of the same name by Yoshitoki Ōima. A grade-school student with a hearing impairment is bullied and transfers to another school. Years later, the former bully is tormented by his behaviour and sets out to make amends.

I Want To Eat Your Pancreas


is a 2018 Japanese animated coming-of-age drama film by Yoru Sumino. An aloof boy comes across a book in a hospital waiting room. He soon discovers that it is a diary kept by his very popular classmate who reveals to him that she is secretly suffering from a fatal pancreatic illness.

Her Blue Sky


is a 2019 Japanese animated film produced by CloverWorks. The film is directed by Tatsuyuki Nagai and written by Mari Okada, Her older sister Akane's ex-boyfriend Shinnosuke Kanomura is a struggling guitarist. Aoi and Akane's parents passed away in an accident 13 years ago, and Akane gave up her ambition of going to Tokyo with Shinnosuke to take care of Aoi.

Kaguya Sama: The First Kiss That Never Ends


is a Japanese romantic comedy anime film by Aka Akasaka, Following the events of the third season, Kaguya Shinomiya agrees to accompany Miyuki Shirogane to America for school, giving him a kiss in the process. Later that night, Kaguya is horrified to learn from her maid, Ai Hayasaka, that she took it one step too far by accidentally giving Shirogane “an adult kiss”.

Maquia: When The Promised Flower Blooms


The people of Iolph are known for two things: their youthful longevity and peaceful lives weaving tapestry. When that is disturbed and their home thrown into chaos by those believing that their blood gifts longer life, Maquia loses not only her friends, but a place to return to.

Tenki No Ko


is a 2019 Japanese animated romantic fantasy film written and directed by Makoto Shinkai. Set during a period of exceptionally rainy weather, high-school boy Hodaka Morishima runs away from his troubled rural home to Tokyo and befriends an orphan girl who can manipulate the weather.
